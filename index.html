Message from Head of Department

Chuka university office of career services is a relatively newly established office. Dr. Joyce Mghoi Macharia is the director working with two other assistants; Mr. Morris Mwingi and Mrs Catherine Munene. To achieve our mandate, the Office of career services will offer:

- Career Counselling services

- Graduate tracker services and Alumni network

- Linkage between industries and students

- High school career fairs and recruitment

- Intra-institutional exchange

- Apprenticeship services

- Create and encourage entrepreneur incubator


You are all welcome to our office


Ms. Joyce Macharia
